Where certain vital information goes missing, it can affect forensic result and generate inaccurate conclusions during investigations. The remedy for this can be to add new knowledge-data, although this may be time-consuming.

Limits of AI systems

Its impossible to use expert systems for large amounts of data. On the other hand, pattern Recognitions solutions can generate vast number of false positives and false negatives. They are computationally intensive and can cause a burned-on computing resource.


Current Problems

1. Scalability

This refers to issue around volumes of data in digital forensics. The volumes and complexity of data are on the rise, which can create problems for investigators.


2. Data encryption and hiding

This issue has cropped up to create a problem for investigator who require the hidden data to gather evidence.


3. Evidence Validation

Nowadays the authenticity and methods used to collect criminal evidence in digital forensics are often being called to question and vehemently opposed in courts.


4. Lack of hardware equipment

Equipment used in digital forensics is usually expensive and hard to come by and this create a problem.
